About MPN

MPN (My Package Manager) is a development platform and package manager designed for building and managing applications, similar to Node.js. It simplifies project setup and management, making it easier for developers to work with various templates and packages. MPN provides a range of templates to kickstart your projects.

Available Templates

MPN offers various templates that you can use to bootstrap your projects. Below are some of the popular templates available:

  • Svelte Template
  • React Template
  • Next.js Template
